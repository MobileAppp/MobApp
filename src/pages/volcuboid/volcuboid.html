Volume of a cuboid

The volume of cuboid is found by multiplying the length by the height by the base

Thus,


Volume = height(h) x length(l) x width(w)



Example 1:
Find the volume of a cuboid with length 3cm, height 4cm and width 5cm
Solution
volume = l x h x w
Volume = 3 x 4 x 3
volume = 60 cubic cm

Example 2:
Find the volume of a cuboid with length 5cm, height 4cm and width 6cm
Solution
volume = l x h x w
Volume = 5 x 4 x 6
volume = 120 cubic cm
