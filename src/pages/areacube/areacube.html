Area of a Cube

For cube, length=breadth = height
Suppose length of an edge = l
Hence, surface area of the cube

= 2(l x l +l x l + l x l)

= 2 x (3l²) = 6l²


Example 1:
Calulate the area of cube with lengh 3cm
Solution:
Area of the cube = 6l², l = 3cm
Area of the cube = 6(3)²
Area of the cube = 6(9)
Area of the cube = 54cm²

Example 2:
Calulate the area of cube with lengh 4cm
Solution:
Area of the cube = 6l², l = 4cm
Area of the cube = 6(4)²
Area of the cube = 6(16)
Area of the cube = 96cm²
