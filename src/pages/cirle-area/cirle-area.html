Area of a Circle

Area of a circle

FORMULAR:
Area of a circle = π x radius x radius
A = π x r x r
A = πr²
where π = 3.14

ILLUSTRATION:





Example 1:

Find the area of circle with a radius 4cm

Solution:

radius = 4cm, π = 3.14
Area = πr²
Area = 3.14 x 4²
Area = 3.14 x 4 x 4
Area = 50.24cm²

Example 2 :

Find the area of circle with a radius 5m

Solution:

radius = 5m, π = 3.14
Area = πr²
Area = 3.14 x 5²
Area = 3.14 x 5 x 5
Area = 78.5cm²

Example 2 :

Find the area of circle with a diameter of 12m

Solution:


radius = diameter / 2 = 12 /2 = 6 ;
π = 3.14
Area = πr²

Area = 3.14 x 6 x 6
