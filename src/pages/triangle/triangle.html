Triangle

FORMULAR: Area = 1/2 x b x h

Where:

b = base

h = height

ILLUSTRATION

ILLUSTRATION: 2

EXAMPLE 1
The triangle in illustration 2 has a height of 9 mm and a base of 8 mm. Calculate the area.

    SOLUTION:

    Steps:

  • Calculate the area of the first have which will be 1/2 x 1/2 x b

  • Calculate the area of the second half using the same fomula
  • Add the two answeres

    • Area of the first half
    Area = 1/2 x 1/2 x b x h
    Area = 1/2 x 1/2 x 8 x 9
    Area = 36 square mm
    Area of the Second half Area = 1/2 x 1/2 x b x h
    Area = 1/2 x 1/2 x 8 x 9
    Area = 36 square mm
    Total area = Area of first half + Area of the second half
    Total area = 36 square mm + 36 square mm
    Total area = 72 square mm
