Sum of the Interior angle of a regular polygon

Angles in polygons

We can calculate the sum of interior angle of any polygons by splinting into triangles. Remember angles in triangles = 180


If there are n number of sides of a polygon, then there will (n-2) triangle in it.

Therefore the sum of interior angle will be:

(n - 2) x 180

where n = number of sides

EXAMPLE 1:


Calculate the sum of interior angle of a regular poly gon with 5 sides


Solution:

Sum of interior angle = (n - 2) x 180, n = 5

= (5 - 2) x 180

= 3 x 180

= 540


EXAMPLE 2:


Calculate the sum of interior angle of a regular poly gon with 4 sides


Solution:

Sum of interior angle = (n - 2) x 180, n = 4

= (4 - 2) x 180

= 2 x 180

= 360

EXAMPLE 3:


Calculate the number of sides of a regular polygon whose interior angles 720


Solution:

Sum of interior angle = (n - 2) x 180, n = ?, Sum of interior angle = 720

720 = (n - 2) x 180

720 /180 = n - 2

4 = n - 2

n = 4 + 2

n = 6
